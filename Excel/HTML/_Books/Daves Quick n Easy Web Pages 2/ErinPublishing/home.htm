Home Page

Welcome to erinbooks.com, the online home of Erin Publications.

We publish guide books which serve as both textbooks and reference books.

Beginners can use our books as textbooks to learn a subject totally new to them. They can then put that knowledge to use while keeping our books available as reference books to refresh their memory.

Our books are designed for self-study to use but work well in a classroom setting too. They utilize an easy-to-follow format and are written in plain English

All topics are cross-referenced and indexed. Bibliographic referneces list sources of more detailed information on each topic.
