About Us

Erin Publications is a family-owned independent publisher specializing in easy-to-follow text/reference books.

The company got it's start in 1970, publishing books which explain Canadian Customs laws, regulations, and procedures.

Erin is now expanding in a new direction, with a series of guides for beginners which explain how to create and publish web sites on the internet. The first book of this series was released April 1999. It is now in its third edition. The second book was released October 2004.
